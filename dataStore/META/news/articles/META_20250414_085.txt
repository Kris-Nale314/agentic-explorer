Title: Meta resorted to 'buy-or-bury scheme' with Instagram and WhatsApp deals, former FTC Chair Lina Khan says
Date: 2025-04-14 09:49:01
Source: cnbc.com
URL: https://www.cnbc.com/2025/04/14/former-ftc-chair-khan-meta-acquisitions-instagram-whatsapp.html

Former FTC Chair Lina Khan said Monday that Facebook's acquisitions of Instagram and WhatsApp drastically changed the social networking ecosystem. The trial could result in the social media giant Meta divesting the two companies.
