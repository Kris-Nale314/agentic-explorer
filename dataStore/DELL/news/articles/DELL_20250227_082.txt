Title: Dell Technologies Q4 EPS Exceeds Target
Date: 2025-02-27 18:59:00
Source: fool.com
URL: https://www.fool.com/data-news/2025/02/27/dell-technologies-q4-eps-exceeds-target/

Dell Technologies (DELL -6.76%), a leading provider of IT solutions, reported mixed fiscal 2025 fourth-quarter results on Thursday, Feb. 27. Adjusted EPS of $2.68 surpassed analyst's consensus expectations of $2.52.
