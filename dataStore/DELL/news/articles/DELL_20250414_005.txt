Title: Apple, Dell, and Other Tech Stocks Climb on Tariff Exemptions
Date: 2025-04-14 16:51:15
Source: investopedia.com
URL: https://www.investopedia.com/apple-dell-and-other-tech-stocks-climb-on-tariff-exemptions-11714493

Apple (AAPL), Dell Technologies (DELL), and other tech stocks gained Monday, after President Donald Trump imposed a pause on import tariffs on many electronic goods.
