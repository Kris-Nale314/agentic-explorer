Title: Tesla's first-quarter registrations in California fell 15%, industry data shows
Date: 2025-04-16 11:04:26
Source: reuters.com
URL: https://www.reuters.com/business/autos-transportation/teslas-first-quarter-registrations-california-fell-15-industry-data-shows-2025-04-16/

Tesla's electric vehicle registrations in California dropped 15.1% during the first quarter, according to industry data, signaling growing challenges for the Elon Musk-led automaker in the crucial U.S. market.
