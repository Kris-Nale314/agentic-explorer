Title: EV maker Polestar bids to lure disgruntled Tesla owners with discounts
Date: 2025-04-10 09:41:25
Source: reuters.com
URL: https://www.reuters.com/business/autos-transportation/ev-maker-polestar-bids-lure-disgruntled-tesla-owners-with-discounts-2025-04-10/

Polestar is offering discounts of up to $20,000 towards a lease of its Polestar 3 vehicle for current Tesla owners in the United States, as the Swedish EV maker targets drivers shunning CEO Elon Musk's car brand.
