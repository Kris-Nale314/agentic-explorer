Title: Tesla sales slump in California even as car buying surges ahead of tariffs
Date: 2025-04-16 10:03:40
Source: businessinsider.com
URL: https://www.businessinsider.com/tesla-sales-california-pre-tariff-data-2025-4

Tesla registrations in California fell over 15% during the first quarter. It is the sixth consecutive quarter that Tesla registrations have fallen in the state.
