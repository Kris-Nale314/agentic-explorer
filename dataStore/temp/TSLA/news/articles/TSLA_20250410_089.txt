Title: Tesla shares retreat following sharpest rally since 2013
Date: 2025-04-10 16:20:22
Source: cnbc.com
URL: https://www.cnbc.com/2025/04/10/tesla-shares-retreat-following-sharpest-rally-since-2013.html

Tesla shares have been on a downward trajectory this year, but the stock soared on Wednesday after President Trump announced a 90-day pause on most new tariffs. But the stock dropped 7.3% on Thursday as part of a broader market selloff.
