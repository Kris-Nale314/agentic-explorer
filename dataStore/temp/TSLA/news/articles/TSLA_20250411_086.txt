Title: Tesla suspends taking new orders for Model S and Model X on Chinese website
Date: 2025-04-11 00:06:29
Source: reuters.com
URL: https://www.reuters.com/business/autos-transportation/tesla-suspends-taking-new-orders-model-s-model-x-chinese-website-2025-04-11/

Tesla has suspended taking new orders for Model S and Model X vehicles on its Chinese website, according to checks made by Reuters on Friday.
