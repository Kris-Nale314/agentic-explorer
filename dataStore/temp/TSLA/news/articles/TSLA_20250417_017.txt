Title: Tesla drops Cybertruck production targets and moves some workers off the line, employees say
Date: 2025-04-17 04:15:02
Source: businessinsider.com
URL: https://www.businessinsider.com/tesla-cybertruck-production-drops-sales-slump-2025-4

Tesla is reducing Cybertruck production and reallocating some staff to Model Y lines, workers say. Tesla has delivered fewer than 50,000 Cybertrucks, according to a March 20 recall.
