Title: Tesla speeds up odometers to avoid warranty repairs, US lawsuit claims
Date: 2025-04-17 17:35:50
Source: reuters.com
URL: https://www.reuters.com/business/autos-transportation/tesla-speeds-up-odometers-avoid-warranty-repairs-us-lawsuit-claims-2025-04-17/

Tesla faces a proposed class action claiming it speeds up odometers on its electric vehicles so they fall out of warranty faster, saving Elon Musk's company from having to pay for repairs.
