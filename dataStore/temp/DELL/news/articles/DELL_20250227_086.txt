Title: Dell forecasts $15 billion of AI server sales this year
Date: 2025-02-27 16:58:49
Source: cnbc.com
URL: https://www.cnbc.com/2025/02/27/dell-earnings-report-q4-2025-.html

Dell missed on sales but beat on earnings for the fourth quarter. The company said it sold about $10 billion of AI-optimized servers in its fiscal 2025, and expects to sell about $15 billion in AI system in the current year.
