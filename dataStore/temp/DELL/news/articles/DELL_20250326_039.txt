Title: Dell's staff numbers have dropped by 25,000 in just 2 years
Date: 2025-03-26 12:52:40
Source: businessinsider.com
URL: https://www.businessinsider.com/dell-employee-numbers-decline-by-25000-since-2023-rto-layoffs-2025-3

An SEC filing shows Dell's head count has fallen by 12,000 in the last year and 25,000 in the past two. Since February 2023, the tech company's employee numbers have fallen by almost 19%.
