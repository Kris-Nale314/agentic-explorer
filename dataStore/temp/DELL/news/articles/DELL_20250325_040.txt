Title: Dell's workforce drops 10% in fiscal 2025
Date: 2025-03-25 18:08:43
Source: reuters.com
URL: https://www.reuters.com/technology/dells-workforce-drops-10-fiscal-2025-2025-03-25/

Dell Technologies headcount fell by 10% in fiscal 2025, and it reaffirmed its commitment to diversity and inclusion, the AI server maker said on Tuesday.
