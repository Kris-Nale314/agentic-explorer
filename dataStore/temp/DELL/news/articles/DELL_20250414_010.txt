Title: Apple, Dell, and Other Tech Stocks Surge on Tariff Exemptions
Date: 2025-04-14 08:41:03
Source: investopedia.com
URL: https://www.investopedia.com/apple-dell-and-other-tech-stocks-surge-on-tariff-exemptions-11714493

Apple (AAPL), Dell Technologies (DELL), and other tech stocks surged in premarket trading Monday, after President Donald Trump imposed a pause on import tariffs on many electronic goods.
