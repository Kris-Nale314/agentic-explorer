Title: Singapore charges men with defrauding Dell, Super Micro
Date: 2025-03-06 00:51:29
Source: reuters.com
URL: https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/singapore-adds-charges-two-held-server-fraud-case-2025-03-06/

Singapore authorities added further charges on Thursday against three men remanded in a case that is part of a wider police investigation into server fraud that may contain AI chips, court documents showed.
