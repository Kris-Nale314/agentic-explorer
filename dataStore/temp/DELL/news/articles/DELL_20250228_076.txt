Title: Dell shares fall on mixed Q4 report, conservative guidance
Date: 2025-02-28 09:58:09
Source: proactiveinvestors.com
URL: https://www.proactiveinvestors.com/companies/news/1067154

Dell Technologies Inc (NASDAQ:DELL) shares fell in early trade on Friday after the computer hardware and software firm reported mixed results for the fourth quarter and guidance below expectations. Q4 earnings of $1.91 billion or $2.68 per share were ahead of Wall Street estimates of $2.52 but revenue of $23.9 billion missed the consensus $24.6 billion.
