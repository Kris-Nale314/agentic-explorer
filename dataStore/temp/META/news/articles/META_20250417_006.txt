Meta Platforms (META) faces an ongoing legal battle, defending itself from the US Federal Trade Commission's claims that it holds an illegal monopoly. Cuneo Gilbert & LaDuca Public Policy and Legislative Affairs practice chair Slade Bond joins Catalysts with Madison Mills and Citi's head of US equity trading strategy, Stuart Kaiser, to discuss the Meta case and how it could shape antitrust regulation in the US.
