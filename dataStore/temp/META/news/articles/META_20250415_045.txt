Mark Zuckerberg defended Meta's (META) past acquisitions in court, arguing Instagram and WhatsApp strengthened competition rather than stifled it. Mozelle Thompson, former FTC commissioner and former Facebook Advisory Board member, joins Morning Brief to break down how the FTC is framing Meta's moves as monopolistic and what the outcome could mean.
