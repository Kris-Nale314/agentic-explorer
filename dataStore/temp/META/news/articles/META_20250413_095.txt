Title: U.S. Prepares to Challenge Meta's Social Media Dominance
Date: 2025-04-13 05:00:22
Source: nytimes.com
URL: https://www.nytimes.com/2025/04/13/technology/meta-ftc-trial-whatsapp-instagram.html
