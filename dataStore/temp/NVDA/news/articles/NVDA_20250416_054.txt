Title: Nvidia probed over how its chips may have been obtained by DeepSeek, which US lawmakers accused of spying for China
Date: 2025-04-16 19:13:19
Source: businessinsider.com
URL: https://www.businessinsider.com/lawmakers-deepseek-spying-china-nvidia-probed-chips-2025-4

US lawmakers are looking into how DeepSeek may have gotten Nvidia chips despite export controls. They also accused DeepSeek of funneling American user data to the Chinese government.
