Title: Chip Shares Tumble After Nvidia Discloses Export Restrictions
Date: 2025-04-16 10:02:00
Source: wsj.com
URL: https://www.wsj.com/tech/chip-shares-tumble-after-nvidia-discloses-export-restrictions-b76a6755

Export restrictions placed on certain chips manufactured by Nvidia have weighed on other chipmakers, and shares of Broadcom, Qualcomm and others declined.
