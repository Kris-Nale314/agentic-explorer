Title: Tesla suspends importing Cybercab, Semi parts from China as Trump tariff war wages on: report
Date: 2025-04-16 13:18:02
Source: nypost.com
URL: https://nypost.com/2025/04/16/business/tesla-suspends-importing-cybercab-semi-parts-from-china-amid-trump-tariff-war-report/

Elon Musk's Tesla has reportedly halted imports from China of car parts needed for its upcoming Cybercab and Semi electric truck due to the impact of President Trump's trade war with Beijing.
