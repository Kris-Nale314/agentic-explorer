Title: Trump's tariffs on Chinese parts for Cybercab, Semi disrupt Tesla's US production plans, source says
Date: 2025-04-15 21:35:58
Source: reuters.com
URL: https://www.reuters.com/business/autos-transportation/trumps-tariffs-chinese-parts-cybercab-semi-disrupt-teslas-us-production-plans-2025-04-16/

Tesla 's plans to ship components from China for Cybercab and Semi electric trucks in the United States were suspended after President Donald Trump raised tariffs on Chinese goods amid a trade war, said a person with direct knowledge.
