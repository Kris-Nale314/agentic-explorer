Title: Tesla Deletes Buy Option for Model S and X From China Website
Date: 2025-04-11 05:20:00
Source: wsj.com
URL: https://www.wsj.com/business/autos/tesla-deletes-buy-option-for-model-s-and-x-from-china-website-a6612085

Tesla has removed the option for purchases of new Model S and Model X vehicles on its China website as trade tensions between the world's two largest economies escalate.
